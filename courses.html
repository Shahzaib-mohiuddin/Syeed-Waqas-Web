Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Get answers to common questions about our courses, enrollment, and learning approach

Which course is right for me?

The right course depends on your current skill level and goals. If you're starting from basics, begin with Algebra or Geometry. For SAT prep or college readiness, Pre Calculus is ideal. If you're preparing for AP exams, choose our Calculus AB & BC course. We offer a free assessment to help determine the best fit for you.

How long does each course take to complete?

Course duration varies: Algebra and Geometry run 12 weeks (24 lessons), Pre Calculus is 14 weeks (28 lessons), and Calculus AB & BC is 16 weeks (32 lessons). Each lesson is 2 hours, with additional practice time recommended between sessions.

Are classes live or pre-recorded?

All courses feature live, interactive sessions with Dr. Syed via Zoom. This allows for real-time questions, personalized feedback, and collaborative problem-solving. Sessions are recorded and available for review, but live attendance is strongly encouraged for maximum benefit.

What materials are included?

Each course includes comprehensive study materials: digital textbooks, practice problem sets, video lesson recordings, formula sheets, and access to our online learning platform. For AP courses, you'll also receive official practice exams and test-taking strategies.

What is the class size?

We maintain small class sizes of 8-12 students maximum to ensure personalized attention. This allows Dr. Syed to address individual questions, track each student's progress, and adapt teaching methods to the group's needs.

Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?

Yes! In addition to group courses, we offer private one-on-one tutoring sessions for students who need intensive, personalized support or have specific learning goals. Contact us for pricing and availability.

What if I miss a class?

All live sessions are recorded and available within 24 hours. You can watch the recording at your convenience and email Dr. Syed with any questions. However, we recommend attending live whenever possible for the interactive learning experience.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied after the first week, you'll receive a full refund. After that period, refunds are prorated based on remaining sessions. We're confident you'll love the course!

Do you guarantee score improvements?

While we can't guarantee specific scores (as results depend on individual effort), 98% of our students show significant improvement. For SAT prep students who complete all assignments, we guarantee at least a 150-point improvement or provide additional sessions free of charge.
