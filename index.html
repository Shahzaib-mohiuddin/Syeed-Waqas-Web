What is the average score improvement for your students?

Our students typically see an average improvement of 150-200 points on their SAT math scores. Many students achieve even higher results, with some improving by over 300 points.

How long are the tutoring sessions?

Standard sessions are 2 hours long. We offer flexible scheduling options including 1-hour intensive sessions and extended 3-hour marathon sessions for comprehensive review.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes! All our tutoring sessions are conducted online via Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet. We use digital whiteboards and screen sharing for an interactive learning experience.

What materials are included in the courses?

All courses include practice tests, video lessons, formula sheets, study guides, and access to our online learning platform with additional resources.

Is there a score improvement guarantee?

Yes! We guarantee at least a 150-point improvement in your SAT math score. If you don't reach this target, we'll provide additional sessions free of charge.

How do I schedule my first session?

Simply fill out the contact form on our website or call us directly. We'll schedule a free consultation to assess your needs and create a personalized learning plan.
