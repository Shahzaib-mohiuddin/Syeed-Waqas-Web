Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

Can I switch plans after starting?

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time. The remaining balance from your current plan will be credited toward the new one.

What is included in a live session?

Each live session is a 2-hour one-on-one video call with Dr. Syed where you work through problems, learn strategies, and get personalized feedback on your progress.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the program after your first week, we'll provide a full refund — no questions asked.

How do I schedule my sessions?

After enrollment, you'll receive a scheduling link where you can book sessions at times that work for you. Flexible scheduling is available 7 days a week.

What does "Score Guarantee" mean?

With the Intensive Prep plan, if your SAT math score doesn't improve by at least 100 points, you'll receive additional sessions at no extra cost until you reach your goal.

Are sessions online or in-person?

All sessions are conducted online via Zoom. This gives students worldwide access to Dr. Syed's expertise from the comfort of their own home.
